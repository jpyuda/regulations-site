About eRegulations

eRegulations is a web-based application that makes regulations easy to read, find and understand. Something about related information, regulation timeline and easy navigation. It is continually being developed by the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau, and is a public domain work of the United States Government.

Developers

eRegulations is an open source project. Visit eregs.github.io to learn how you can contribute.
